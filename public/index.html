About the ICICFT–2026 Conference

We are pleased to announce the upcoming International Conference on Intelligent Computing and Future Technologies (ICICFT-2026), which aims to promote high-quality research and real-world technological impact through international collaboration. This conference will bring together world-class researchers, academicians, industry experts, and technology leaders to explore and discuss the latest developments and innovations in the fields of Intelligent Computing and emerging digital technologies.


The primary objective of ICICFT-2026 is to create a global platform where researchers and professionals from different parts of the world can come together to exchange knowledge, present innovative research findings, and build strong professional networks. Through such interactions, the conference seeks to encourage meaningful collaborations and open new opportunities for interdisciplinary research and technological innovation.

Conference Tracks

ICICFT-2026 features five major research tracks covering key areas of intelligent computing, data science, cybersecurity, emerging technologies, and real-world applications of AI across healthcare and society.

Artificial Intelligence and Intelligent Systems

Focuses on the development of intelligent algorithms and systems capable of learning, reasoning, and making autonomous decisions for real-world applications.

Key Areas: Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Natural Language Processing.

Data Science, Analytics and Intelligent Decision Systems

Explores advanced methods for extracting meaningful insights from complex datasets and building data-driven decision systems across industries.

Key Areas: Big Data Analytics, Data Mining, Predictive Analytics.

Cyber Security, Networks and Distributed Computing

Addresses modern challenges in securing digital infrastructure, communication networks, and distributed computing environments.

Key Areas: Network Security, Cryptography, Cloud Computing.

Internet of Things, Smart Systems and Emerging Technologies

Focuses on interconnected smart systems and next-generation technologies that enable intelligent automation and smart environments.

Key Areas: Internet of Things (IoT), Robotics and Automation, Smart Cities.

Intelligent Computing Applications in Healthcare and Society

Highlights the application of intelligent computing solutions to improve healthcare systems and address broader societal challenges.

Key Areas: AI in Healthcare, Medical Image Processing, Health Informatics.
