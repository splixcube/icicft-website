What is ICSCIS–2026?

The International Conference on Sustainable Computing and Intelligent Systems (ICSCIS-2026) is a premier academic and research platform committed to advancing innovation in intelligent technologies and sustainable digital ecosystems. The conference is designed to address the growing demand for energy-efficient, scalable, and intelligent solutions that can support the rapidly evolving global digital landscape.

ICSCIS-2026 brings together researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and policy experts to explore transformative developments in Artificial Intelligence, Internet of Things, Big Data Analytics, Intelligent Automation, Cyber-Physical Systems, Cloud and Edge Computing, and Green Computing technologies. The conference emphasizes sustainable design principles, responsible innovation, and technology frameworks that balance performance, efficiency, and societal impact.

Topics @ ICSCSIS–2026

ICSCIS-2026 covers interdisciplinary research areas that address emerging challenges in sustainable computing, intelligent systems, and digital transformation.

Smart and Autonomous Systems

Development of intelligent systems that can perceive, decide, and act independently. Applications include autonomous vehicles, robotics, and adaptive industrial systems.

Big Data Analytics

Analysis of large and complex datasets to discover patterns, trends, and insights. Applications include predictive analytics, data-driven decision making, and real-time processing.

Drones

Advancements in unmanned aerial vehicles for surveillance, mapping, and delivery. Research focuses on navigation, swarm coordination, and real-time data collection.

Artificial Intelligence

Development of intelligent systems that can learn, reason, and make decisions. Applications include machine learning, deep learning, computer vision, and natural language processing.
