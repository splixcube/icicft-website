Paper Submission Guidelines

  • • Kindly use the Full Paper Template provided below.
  • • Paper must be original and not under review elsewhere.
  • • Plagiarism must be below 10%.
  • • AI-generated content similarity must be 0%.
  • • All submissions will be screened using Turnitin plagiarism detection software.
  • • Papers failing to meet originality standards will be rejected.
  • • Submit full article in DOC or DOCX format.
  • • Ensure clear professional English language.

At least one author must register. Only registered and presented papers will be processed for publication. Maximum six authors per paper.

How to Submit?

Authors must submit full-length manuscripts via Microsoft CMT or through Google Form.

Microsoft CMT

Only full-length papers are accepted for review.

Review Process

All papers below 10% similarity will undergo double-blind review.

  • • Accepted for oral presentation
  • • Accepted with minor corrections
  • • Rejected
