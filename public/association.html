1st International Conference on Intelligent Systems and Emerging Technologies

Conference Introduction

We are pleased to announce the upcoming ICISET-2026 (International Conference on Intelligent Systems and Emerging Technologies), which aims to promote high-quality research and real-world technological impact through international collaboration. This conference will bring together world-class researchers, academicians, industry experts, and technology leaders to explore and discuss the latest developments and innovations in the fields of Intelligent Computing and emerging digital technologies.

The primary objective of ICISET-2026 is to create a global platform where researchers and professionals from different parts of the world can come together to exchange knowledge, present innovative research findings, and build strong professional networks. Through such interactions, the conference seeks to encourage meaningful collaborations and open new opportunities for interdisciplinary research and technological innovation.

ICISET-2026 is designed as an inclusive and interdisciplinary event that welcomes participants from diverse research domains. The conference encourages scholars and practitioners to share their ideas, research outcomes, and technological advancements, thereby promoting the cross-fertilization of knowledge across different disciplines. By bringing together experts from academia, industry, and research institutions, the conference aims to bridge the gap between theoretical research and practical applications.

The conference will focus on key areas of intelligent computing and advanced technologies, including Artificial Intelligence and Intelligent Systems, Data Science and Advanced Analytics, Cyber Security and Distributed Computing, Internet of Things and Smart Technologies, Intelligent Computing Applications in Healthcare, and Society and Indian Knowledge System. These thematic tracks are designed to address contemporary technological challenges while promoting innovative solutions that can contribute to sustainable development and digital transformation.

One of the central goals of ICISET-2026 is to encourage the integration of knowledge from different research domains and facilitate the application of computing technologies to solve real-world problems. By combining expertise from multiple disciplines, the conference aims to inspire new perspectives, foster technological breakthroughs, and support the development of next-generation intelligent systems.

We warmly invite researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and students from across the globe to actively participate in ICISET-2026. This conference offers an excellent opportunity to present your research, learn from leading experts, exchange ideas with peers, and explore potential collaborations. Your participation and contributions will play a significant role in advancing research and shaping the future of intelligent computing and future technologies.

About Poornima University

Poornima University (PU) is a state private University in Jaipur, India established by an act of the Rajasthan legislature. PU was established in 2012 with a vision to create a knowledge-based society with scientific temper, team spirit and dignity of labour to face global competition and challenges. With modern infrastructure, strong industry collaborations, and emphasis on interdisciplinary research, Poornima University actively contributes to technological and societal advancement.

PU is recognized by University Grants Commission (UGC) under Section 2(f) and 12(B) with the right to confer degrees as per Section 22 (1) of the UGC Act, 1956. Its architecture program is approved by the Council of Architecture (CoA) and the hotel management program is approved by NCHMCT. Currently, PU has more than 8000 students from all over the country enrolled in nine faculties and fourteen departments of UG/PG and PhD Programs in various areas such as Management, Engineering, Computer Application Sciences, Architecture, Design, Public Health and Hotel Management, etc. The University is committed to fostering research and innovation, and this conference is part of its mission to promote cutting-edge research in sustainable computing and intelligent systems.
