2nd International Conference on Sustainable Computing and Intelligent Systems

Conference Introduction

The 2nd International Conference on Sustainable Computing and Intelligent Systems (ICSCIS-2026) is a premier academic and research platform committed to advancing innovation in intelligent technologies and sustainable digital ecosystems. The conference is designed to address the growing demand for energy-efficient, scalable, and intelligent solutions that can support the rapidly evolving global digital landscape.

ICSCIS-2026 brings together researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and policy experts to explore transformative developments in Artificial Intelligence, Internet of Things, Big Data Analytics, Intelligent Automation, Cyber-Physical Systems, Cloud and Edge Computing, and Green Computing technologies. The conference emphasizes sustainable design principles, responsible innovation, and technology frameworks that balance performance, efficiency, and societal impact.

With the exponential rise of data-driven systems and interconnected infrastructures, the need for intelligent and resilient computing solutions has never been greater. ICSCIS-2026 provides a dynamic forum for presenting cutting-edge research, discussing real-world applications, and examining emerging challenges related to security, scalability, energy optimization, and digital sustainability.

Through keynote addresses by distinguished experts, peer-reviewed paper presentations, panel discussions, workshops, and collaborative networking sessions, the conference aims to foster interdisciplinary research partnerships and encourage industry-academia synergy. By promoting dialogue across diverse technological domains, ICSCIS-2026 aspires to shape the future of sustainable computing and intelligent systems at both national and global levels.

About Poornima University

Poornima University (PU) is a state private University in Jaipur, India established by an act of the Rajasthan legislature. PU was established in 2012 with a vision to create a knowledge-based society with scientific temper, team spirit and dignity of labour to face global competition and challenges.With modern infrastructure, strong industry collaborations, and emphasis on interdisciplinary research, Poornima University actively contributes to technological and societal advancement.

PU is recognized by University Grants Commission (UGC) under Section 2(f) and 12(B) with the right to confer degrees as per Section 22 (1) of the UGC Act, 1956. Its architecture program is approved by the Council of Architecture (CoA) and the hotel management program is approved by NCHMCT.Currently, PU has more than 8000+ students from all over the country enrolled in nine faculties and fourteen departments of UG/PG and PhD Programs in various areas such as Management, Engineering, Computer Application Sciences, Architecture, Design, Public Health and Hotel Management, etc.The university is committed to fostering research and innovation, and this conference is part of its mission to promote cutting-edge research in sustainable computing and intelligent systems.

About NITTTR

National Institute of Technical Teachers' Training and Research (NITTTR), Bhopal is a premier institution established in 1965 by the Ministry of Education, Government of India, for teacher training and improving the overall quality of the Technical Education System across the country. The Institute has developed expertise through continuous research and development in areas such as competency development in emerging technologies, subject matter enhancement, classroom and laboratory-based learning, industry-institute internships, management of technical institutions, personality development, entrepreneurship development, examination reforms, and student assessment.

It also specializes in instructional resource development, multimedia and video production, educational project planning and management, community development initiatives, and evaluation of programs and projects within the technical education ecosystem. In 2003, the Government of India upgraded the Institute’s mandate, enabling it to function as a national-level resource institution beyond regional boundaries. With modern infrastructure and updated resources, NITTTR continues to support quality enhancement in technical education through strategic collaboration and networking with centers of excellence.
