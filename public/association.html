About the Conference

The 1st International Conference on Intelligent Systems and Emerging Technologies (ICISET–2026) is envisioned as a premier global forum dedicated to advancing research, innovation, and collaboration in the rapidly evolving domains of intelligent computing and emerging digital technologies. The conference aims to promote high-quality research and foster real-world technological impact through meaningful international engagement among academia, industry, and research communities.

ICISET–2026 will bring together distinguished researchers, academicians, industry experts, and technology leaders from across the globe to share knowledge, present novel research findings, and discuss recent advancements shaping the future of intelligent systems. It provides a dynamic platform for insightful discussions, idea exchange, and exploration of emerging trends addressing contemporary technological and societal challenges.

A key objective of the conference is to create an inclusive and interdisciplinary environment that encourages collaboration across diverse domains. By integrating perspectives from multiple fields, ICISET–2026 seeks to bridge the gap between theoretical research and practical implementation, enabling the development of innovative and scalable solutions for real-world problems.

The conference emphasizes the application of advanced computational techniques in areas such as data-driven decision-making, secure and distributed systems, smart technologies, healthcare innovations, and sustainable development. It also highlights the importance of integrating modern technological advancements with traditional knowledge systems to promote holistic and sustainable innovation.

Researchers, academicians, professionals, and students are warmly invited to actively participate and contribute to this global knowledge-sharing platform. ICISET–2026 offers an excellent opportunity to showcase research, gain insights from leading experts, and build meaningful collaborations that will shape the future of intelligent computing and emerging technologies.

About Poornima University

Poornima University (PU) is a state private University in Jaipur, India established by an act of the Rajasthan legislature. PU was established in 2012 with a vision to create a knowledge-based society with scientific temper, team spirit and dignity of labour to face global competition and challenges. With modern infrastructure, strong industry collaborations, and emphasis on interdisciplinary research, Poornima University actively contributes to technological and societal advancement.

PU is recognized by University Grants Commission (UGC) under Section 2(f) and 12(B) with the right to confer degrees as per Section 22 (1) of the UGC Act, 1956. Its architecture program is approved by the Council of Architecture (CoA) and the hotel management program is approved by NCHMCT. Currently, PU has more than 8000 students from all over the country enrolled in nine faculties and fourteen departments of UG/PG and PhD Programs in various areas such as Management, Engineering, Computer Application Sciences, Architecture, Design, Public Health and Hotel Management, etc. The University is committed to fostering research and innovation, and this conference is part of its mission to promote cutting-edge research in sustainable computing and intelligent systems.

About Faculty of Computer Engineering (FCE)

The Faculty of Computer Science & Engineering at Poornima University is a dynamic hub of technological learning, innovation, and research excellence. It is committed to shaping future-ready professionals equipped with the skills and mindset required to excel in an increasingly digital world. With a strong emphasis on emerging technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Cyber Security, Cloud Computing, IoT, Blockchain, Data Analytics, DevOps, and Full-Stack Development, the Faculty of CSE integrates scientific knowledge with real-world application.

By fostering interdisciplinary collaboration, research engagement, and ethical computing practices, the Faculty of CSE plays a pivotal role in driving innovation within the university. It aspires to cultivate skilled engineers who can address modern challenges, adapt to evolving technologies, and lead with integrity in global digital environments.

About IKS Cell, Poornima University

The Indian Knowledge System (IKS) Cell serves as a dynamic platform for integrating India’s rich intellectual heritage with contemporary education, research, and innovation. It promotes interdisciplinary engagement with traditional knowledge across diverse domains including science, healthcare, management, sustainability, and governance. The cell actively contributes to curriculum enrichment, research advancement, and scholarly initiatives grounded in indigenous wisdom. Through workshops, conferences, and collaborative programs, it facilitates the meaningful application of ancient knowledge in modern contexts. Emphasizing value-based education, the IKS Cell inspires students and researchers to develop innovative solutions rooted in Indian traditions for contemporary challenges. It strives to build a future-ready academic ecosystem that seamlessly blends cultural depth with global standards of excellence.

About RCAT

Established by the Government of Rajasthan to enhance competency, employability and mobility of science/engineering/technology students, the youth and mid-career professionals of Rajasthan, India and the world looking forward to get trained and inducted into the advanced and emerging tech industry.

Rajasthan Centre of Advanced Technology (R-CAT) operates under the Department of Information Technology & Communication (DoIT&C), Government of Rajasthan. It focuses on advanced skill development, industry-oriented training, emerging technology programs, and enhancing employability across engineering and technical domains.
