Call for Papers

ICISET 2026 invites original research papers

Submission Guidelines

The theme of the conference will be “Intelligent Systems and Emerging Technologies”. Besides contributions addressing the conference theme, ICISET also welcomes authors to submit their original research results in traditional and emerging research areas across a wide range of application and technological domains within the context of intelligent computing and next-generation digital technologies.

The research areas include, but are not limited to the following tracks mentioned below.

Conference Tracks

ICISET-2026 features five focused research tracks covering the frontiers of intelligent computing and emerging digital technologies.

Track 01

Intelligent Systems & Machine Learning

Machine Learning Deep Learning Neural Networks Natural Language Processing Computer Vision Pattern Recognition Expert Systems Cognitive Computing Reinforcement Learning AI Applications in Industry & Society
Track 02

Data Science & Analytics

Big Data Analytics Data Mining Knowledge Discovery in Databases Predictive Analytics Business Intelligence Data Visualization Data Engineering Scientific Computing Information Retrieval Intelligent Data Processing
Track 03

Cybersecurity & Secure Computing

Network Security Cryptography & Encryption Digital Forensics Cloud Computing Distributed & Parallel Computing Wireless Networks Blockchain Security Privacy Preserving Technologies Secure Computing Systems Computer Networks
Track 04

Smart & Emerging Technologies

Internet of Things (IoT) Wireless Sensor Networks Embedded Systems Edge & Fog Computing Robotics & Automation Cyber-Physical Systems Smart Cities Augmented & Virtual Reality Quantum Computing Green & Sustainable Computing
Track 05

Intelligent Healthcare & Biomedical Applications

AI in Healthcare Medical Image Processing Health Informatics Bioinformatics Disease Prediction with ML Smart Healthcare Systems Wearable Health Monitoring Telemedicine & Digital Healthcare Healthcare Data Analytics Intelligent Systems for Agriculture & Education
