Call for Papers

ICISET 2026 invites original research papers

Submission Guidelines

The theme of the conference will be “Intelligent Systems and Emerging Technologies”. Besides contributions addressing the conference theme, ICISET also welcomes authors to submit their original research results in traditional and emerging research areas across a wide range of application and technological domains within the context of intelligent computing and next-generation digital technologies.

The research areas include, but are not limited to the following tracks mentioned below.

Conference Tracks

ICISET-2026 features five focused research tracks covering the frontiers of intelligent computing and emerging digital technologies.

Track 01

Intelligent Systems & Machine Learning

Advanced Deep Learning and Foundation Models Reinforcement Learning and Autonomous Decision Systems Explainable, Ethical, and Responsible AI Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition Systems Natural Language Processing and Conversational AI Edge AI and Distributed Intelligent Systems Hybrid AI: Neuro-Symbolic and Cognitive Architectures AI for Robotics and Intelligent Automation
Track 02

Cybersecurity & Secure Computing

Zero Trust Architecture and Secure System Design Cryptography, Post-Quantum Security, and Privacy Techniques Blockchain and Decentralized Security Frameworks AI and Machine Learning for Cyber Threat Detection Cloud, Edge, and IoT Security Challenges Digital Forensics and Cybercrime Investigation Secure Software Engineering and DevSecOps Privacy-Preserving Computing and Data Protection
Track 03

Intelligent Healthcare Systems & Biomedical Applications

AI in Medical Imaging and Diagnostics Precision Medicine and Personalized Healthcare Systems Wearable Devices and Remote Patient Monitoring Biomedical Signal and Image Processing Clinical Decision Support Systems and Health Informatics Robotics and Automation in Healthcare AI for Drug Discovery and Genomics Smart Healthcare Systems and Hospital Automation
Track 04

Indian Knowledge Systems & Indigenous Technologies

Digital Preservation and Analysis of Indian Knowledge Systems AI Applications in Sanskrit and Ancient Text Processing Indigenous Technologies for Sustainable Development Traditional Medicine Systems (Ayurveda, Yoga) and Modern Integration Indian Architecture, Vastu, and Sustainable Design Ethnoscience, Agriculture, and Rural Innovation Systems Philosophical Foundations and Computational Perspectives of IKS Cultural Heritage Technologies and Digital Humanities
Track 05

Smart & Emerging Technologies for Sustainable Development

Smart Cities and Intelligent Urban Infrastructure Internet of Things (IoT) for Sustainable Applications Green Computing and Energy-Efficient Systems Renewable Energy Technologies and Smart Grids AI and Data Analytics for Climate Change Mitigation Sustainable Transportation and Mobility Systems Industry 4.0 and Smart Manufacturing Circular Economy and Technology-Driven Sustainability
